Fadiman Protocol

1 day on ¬∑ 2 days off

Most widely used. Developed by Dr. James Fadiman. Allows full tolerance reset between doses.

Stamets Stack

4 days on ¬∑ 3 days off

Combines psilocybin mushrooms + Lion's Mane + Niacin (B3). Some evidence of psychomotor benefits in older adults.

Every Other Day

1 day on ¬∑ 1 day off

Less common, higher frequency. May accelerate tolerance buildup.
