Microdosing Studies

StudyYearKey Finding
Rootman et al.
Scientific Reports		 2022 Largest prospective study (n=1,133). Small-to-medium improvements in mood, depression, anxiety, stress. Psychomotor gains in 55+ adults. Stamets Stack boosted motor performance.
Anderson et al.
Harm Reduction Journal		 2019 Codebook from 278 microdosers. Top benefit: mood (26.6%), focus (14.8%). Top challenge: discomfort (18%), anxiety (6.7%).
Dressler, Bright & Polito
J. Psychedelic Studies		 2021 Increased conscientiousness and emotional insight in prospective psilocybin microdosers.
Polito & Liknaitzky
Systematic Review		 2022 Comprehensive review highlighting importance of separating pharmacological effects from expectancy/placebo.

Preclinical & Animal Research

AreaFinding
Neurogenesis Psilocybin promotes new neural connections and enhances synaptic plasticity in rodent models.
Anti-Inflammation 5-HT2A agonists reduce TNF-alpha-mediated inflammation at sub-hallucinogenic doses in mice (Nichols et al., 2017).
Alzheimer's Psilocybin studied in preclinical Alzheimer's models for anti-inflammatory and neuroplastic effects.
Lion's Mane Synergy Hericium erinaceus promotes nerve growth factor (NGF) and neurogenesis in animal models ‚Äî rationale for "stacking."
Safety No significant organ toxicity in chronic rodent studies. Lowest-harm recreational substance in comparative assessments.
